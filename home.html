About the band

Tors are a three-piece indie folk band from Devon, United Kingdom, composed of brothers Matt and Theo Weedon, and Jack Bowden. They have different roles in the band which are Matt Weedon (lead vocals, guitar), Theo Weedon (guitar, backing vocals), Jack Bowden (drums, piano, banjo, backing vocals).

News

Tors are planning to tour the UK, US, and mainland Europe in the summer and autumn of 2024. They'll be joined by @heythereitsber on their North American tour. THey also released their latest EP, Miracle, in 2024
